Feedback

We are very interested in your feedback. If you find anything in this manual that you consider wrong, ill-formulated, or explained in too difficult a manner, please don't hesitate to contact us at <info@trolltech.com>. Of course, you can also contact us with praise, suggestions for further editions of both the manual and Qt Designer itself, or anything else that you would like to comment on in the world of Qt. Bug reports should be sent to <qt-bugs@trolltech.com>.

If you hold a commercial Qt license, you can also use the support channels that were assigned to you when you bought your license.
